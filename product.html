How to Make a Delicious Cake

Baking a cake is simple and fun! First, gather your ingredients: flour, sugar, eggs, butter, baking powder, vanilla essence, and milk.
Start by mixing the butter and sugar until it becomes light and fluffy.
Then, add the eggs one by one, followed by the vanilla essence. Next, sift the flour and baking powder together and slowly mix it into the batter, adding milk little by little.
Once the batter is smooth, pour it into a greased cake tin. Preheat the oven to 180°C and bake for about 30-40 minutes. After baking, let the cake cool before decorating it with frosting or fresh fruits.
Enjoy your homemade, delicious cake!
